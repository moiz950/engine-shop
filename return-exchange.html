Our Policies

We want you to love your purchase. If something isn't right, we're here to help.

Return Policy

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery for most items. To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in its original condition, and with all tags attached.

  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Items must be unworn and unwashed
  • Original tags must be attached
  • Return shipping is free for defective items
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

Exchange Policy

If you need a different size or color, we offer free exchanges within 30 days of delivery. Exchanges are subject to product availability and will be processed once the returned item is received.

  • Free size and color exchanges
  • Subject to product availability
  • Exchange initiated within 30 days
  • Original packaging required
  • Expedited exchange option available

Damaged / Incorrect Products

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product, please contact us immediately. We'll arrange a free pickup and send a replacement or issue a full refund as per your preference.

  • Report within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of damage for verification
  • Free return pickup arranged
  • Immediate replacement or full refund
  • We cover all return shipping costs

Refund Information

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. You'll receive an email confirmation once your refund has been initiated.

  • Processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunded to original payment method
  • Email confirmation sent
  • Shipping costs non-refundable
  • Contact support for delays

Need Help?

Our customer support team is ready to assist you with any questions about returns, exchanges, or refunds.
